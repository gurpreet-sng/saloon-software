Our software generates a daily summary report that provides you with detailed information on your salon's appointments, collections, sales, and client visits. This report is delivered to your email every EOD, making it easy for you to stay on top of your salon's operations.

Beauty Product Usage Report

Our software provides a beauty product usage report that allows you to track the usage of your salon's beauty products. This report helps you understand which products are popular among your clients and which ones are not, allowing you to make informed decisions about your inventory and product offerings.

Service Provider Work Schedules and Commission Reports

Our software provides detailed reports on your service provider work schedules and commission earnings. This feature allows you to track your service provider's performance and ensure that they are being fairly compensated for their hard work.
