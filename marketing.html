Here Are Some of The Key Features of Our Marketing Tools

Reward Points

Our reward points system allows you to incentivize repeat business by offering points for every purchase. Customers can then redeem these points for discounts on future services.

Memberships

Our membership program allows you to offer your customers exclusive discounts, free services, and other perks in exchange for a monthly or yearly fee. This is a great way to build loyalty and increase customer retention.

Automatic Service Reminders

Our automated service reminders help you keep track of upcoming appointments and send reminders to customers via email or SMS. This ensures that your customers never miss an appointment and helps reduce no-shows.

E-wallet

Our e-wallet feature allows customers to store credit on their account that can be used for future services. This is a convenient way to pay for services without the need for cash or cards.

Bulk SMS & Email

Our bulk SMS and email feature allows you to send promotional messages and appointment reminders to your entire customer base at once. This is a great way to stay top-of-mind and keep customers engaged with your salon.

Coupons on Online Appointments

Our coupon feature allows you to offer discounts on services booked online. This is a great way to incentivize customers to book online and increase your online bookings.
