Our software integrates with Ewallet and reward points systems, allowing your clients to pay for their services and products using their Ewallet balance or reward points. Our software supports online payments, allowing your clients to pay for their services and products directly through your website. We integrate with a range of popular payment gateways, making it easy for your clients to pay using their preferred method.
